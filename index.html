Important Instructions

  • Before applying, make sure you have read and understood all the contents of the University website ( www.onlineuu.com) and Prospectus.
  • The application form has different sections and is designed to get to know the applicant. You are advised to carefully and thoughtfully fill all the sections.
  • Make sure that you provide your authentic e-mail ID and correct phone number which should be functional during the registration process as all communication will be shared through SMS/ Emails.
  • Name and other details entered in the application form must be correct and as per your educational qualification documents.
  • Registration Fee (Rs. 500) is non-refundable and not part of programme fee.
  • The applicant should attach all mandatory documents to prevent the application form from being considered ‘incomplete’. The applicant’s photograph, government identification document, and mark-sheets should be clearly visible and not password-protected.

You would be required to upload the following documents to complete your admission process:

  1. Scanned Passport size Colored Photograph
  2. Scanned Signature
  3. Scanned Copy(ies) of Qualification Documents starting from 10th to highest qualification
  4. Scanned Copy(ies) of Diploma/ Degree of Graduation/ Post Graduation/ PG Diploma or equivalent as applicable
  5. Scanned Copy of Valid ID Proof with Photograph issued by Govt.
  6. Scanned Copy of Residence/ Address proof (if different from ID Proof)
  7. Scanned Copy of Disability Certificate (if applicable)
  8. Scanned Copy of Sample of Handwriting (to be referred in examination)
Note: You need to scan the original documents, not the photocopies.
